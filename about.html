About the Artist

My Story

I am a contemporary artist based in Jhajjar, specializing in mixed media and digital art. My artistic journey began over 15 years ago when I discovered my passion for visual expression through painting.

My work has been featured in galleries across the country and has received recognition for its unique blend of traditional and digital techniques. I draw inspiration from urban landscapes and human emotions, which is reflected in my use of bold colors and dynamic compositions throughout my portfolio. In addition to contemporary themes, I have also painted numerous religious artworks, especially focused on deities like Hanuman and Khatu Shyam.

I believe that art should challenge perspectives and evoke emotion, and I strive to create pieces that resonate with viewers on a personal level. Each artwork represents a chapter in my ongoing exploration of the human experience and our connection to the world around us.

When I'm not in the studio, I enjoy traveling and photographing street art around the world. These experiences often find their way into my work, enriching the narratives and visual language I develop.
